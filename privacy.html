Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Overview

PROOF, LLC (“PROOF,” “we,” “our,” or “us”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the information you share with us.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect information submitted through our website, forms, payment systems, and related services.

Information We Collect

We may collect personal and project-related information including:

  • • Name and contact information
  • • Email address
  • • Project titles and creative materials
  • • Uploaded files and submitted documents
  • • Pitch decks, scripts, references, and related assets
  • • Payment and transaction information
  • • Communication history and support inquiries

How Information Is Used

Information submitted to PROOF may be used to:

  • • Deliver creative development and production services
  • • Review submitted projects and materials
  • • Communicate regarding services, timelines, and revisions
  • • Improve workflows and client experience
  • • Process transactions and onboarding
  • • Maintain internal records and documentation

Payment Processing

Payments are securely processed through third-party payment providers including Stripe.

PROOF does not directly store full payment card information on our servers.

Forms & File Submissions

Project onboarding forms and submitted materials may be collected through third-party tools including Tally, Google Drive, Dropbox, or related collaboration platforms.

Submitted materials are used solely for project development, production, communication, and operational purposes related to your engagement with PROOF.

Communication & Recorded Calls

PROOF may communicate with clients through email, video conferencing platforms, messaging tools, or collaborative software during development and production.

Certain onboarding or development calls may be recorded or transcribed for project documentation, creative alignment, operational clarity, and quality assurance purposes.

Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect submitted information and project materials using commercially reasonable security practices and trusted third-party service providers.

However, no online system can guarantee absolute security.

Third-Party Services

PROOF may use third-party platforms and services including payment processors, analytics providers, hosting services, form tools, communication platforms, and cloud storage providers.

These third-party services may have their own privacy practices and policies.

Cookies & Analytics

Our website may use cookies, analytics tools, and related technologies to improve website functionality, understand visitor behavior, and enhance user experience.

Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of personal information submitted to PROOF, subject to applicable legal and operational obligations.

Changes to This Policy

PROOF may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of the website or services after updates are posted constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
